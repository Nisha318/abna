Membership

The Acadiana Black Nurses Association and the National Black Nurses Association (NBNA) membership fees are due annually.  Membership is active once all membership dues are paid in full.  Members with paid fees are active from January 01 through December 31 of the current paid year.  The membership campaign for 2014 is set and ready to go.  This year’s theme is: “Each One Reach One”.  The campaign will be conducted January 1, 2014 – May 15, 2014.  I challenge each of you to recruit at least one member.
